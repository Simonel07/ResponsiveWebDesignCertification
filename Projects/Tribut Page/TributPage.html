Here we have a short biography of Leonardo da Vinci:

  • 1452 - Was born in Tuscan in 15 April.
  • 1478 - He became an independent master.
  • 1483 - He moved to Milan to work for ruling Sforza family as an engineer, sculptor, painter and architect.
  • 1495-1497 - He produced a mural of "The Last Supper" in the refectory of the Monastery of Santa Maria delle Grazie from Milan.
  • 1499 - When Milan was invaded by French and Sforza family forced to flee, he might visited Venice and then went to Florence.
  • 1499-1506 - During his time in Florence, he painted several portraits, but the only one that survives is the famous "Mona Lisa", painted in 1503-1506.
  • 1506 - He returned to Milan remaining there until 1513.
  • 1517 - Because of the invasion of the French king Francis I, Leonardo moved to Château of Cloux, near Amboise in France.
  • 1519 - Leonardo died on 2 May in Château of Cloux.
